# TOMB — DNA Storage Codec
## The open standard layer the DNA storage industry needs

**Patent pending IN202641065626 · Bengaluru, India · 2026**

---

### The Gap

Every DNA storage company encodes data differently.
No interoperability. No standard. Customers cannot switch vendors.

### TOMB Fills the Gap

TOMB is the codec standard that sits between your application and your DNA hardware.
Any hardware implementing TOMB's 4-method HAL interface is immediately compatible.
One codec. Any hardware. Forever.
